What We Do

A full spectrum of painting and coating services — from single rooms to entire building envelopes.

01

Residential Interior

Drywall, plaster, woodwork, wallpaper, and custom color matching. From single-room refreshes to full-home transformations with meticulous detail.

02

Residential Exterior

Weather-resistant primers and topcoats engineered for Long Island's coastal climate. Siding, trim, and architectural detail finishing built to last.

03

Commercial

Office parks, retail, medical facilities, and mixed-use properties. We work around your operations to deliver on time with zero disruption.

04

Industrial Coatings

Epoxy floor systems, polyurethane finishes, synthetic enamels, and lacquers for warehouses, manufacturing, healthcare, and high-traffic environments.

Surface Preparation

Power washing, scraping, sanding, structural repair, and caulking. A finish is only as durable as the surface beneath it — we never skip steps.

Production-style unit turnovers that are fast, consistent, and tenant-ready. Dedicated crews trained for volume without compromising quality.

Color Consultation

Expert palette selection that complements your architecture, lighting, and aesthetic — plus precise custom matching for restoration and brand-standard spaces.

About

We are not a pickup truck and a ladder.

JWK Services is a family operation — built by Warren Krotz and now run alongside his son Jake out of Huntington, New York. What began as a father-and-son crew has grown into a full-scale professional painting and coatings outfit. We deploy specialized teams — prep, finish, and production — across residential, commercial, and industrial projects of every size. From the Gold Coast to the East End to Manhattan, the Krotz family brings the same standard of precision to every address they serve.
