What We Do

JWK Services Inc. provides expert interior maintenance and trade services for New York's commercial facilities.

01

Commercial & General Maintenance

Daily, weekly, or monthly maintenance plans with 24/7 service. We handle everything: handyman services, artwork & TV hanging, door & ceiling repairs, Sheetrock, Formica, soundproofing, and all flooring types (VCT, carpet tile, ceramic).

02

Surface Prep & Restoration

Water damage cleaning and repairs, 24-hour emergency service, cleanup, restoration, and construction. We never skip steps.

03

Demolition & Carting

Complete demolition and carting services for renovations and clear-outs. Fast, safe, and efficient removal.

04

Residential Interior

Drywall, plaster, woodwork, wallpaper, and custom color matching. Single-room refreshes to full-home transformations with meticulous detail.
